Admissions

Join a world-class Computer Science program with cutting-edge research and career opportunities

Application Process

1

Online Application

Complete our comprehensive online application form with personal information and academic background.

Timeline: Ongoing

2

Document Submission

Submit official transcripts, test scores, resume/CV, statement of purpose, and reference letters.

Deadline: Program Specific

3

Entrance Assessment

Selected candidates take technical aptitude assessment in programming, algorithms, and problem-solving.

Duration: 2 hours

4

Interview

Meet with faculty for in-depth discussion of research interests, academic goals, and motivation.

Format: Online/In-person

5

Admission Decision

Receive final admission decision with details on enrollment, tuition, and financial aid packages.

Detailed Information

Entry Requirements by Program

Bachelor of Science

4 Years • Full-Time

  • • High school diploma or equivalent
  • • SAT: 1100+ or ACT: 24+
  • • GPA: 3.0 or higher
  • • English proficiency (if applicable)
  • • Statement of purpose (250 words)
  • • 2 Recommendation letters

Master of Science

2 Years • Full-Time

  • • Bachelor's in CS or related field
  • • GRE: 310+ (recommended)
  • • Undergraduate GPA: 3.2+
  • • Resume/CV with work experience
  • • Statement of purpose (500 words)
  • • 3 Recommendation letters

Doctor of Philosophy

4-6 Years • Full-Time

  • • Master's degree or equivalent
  • • GRE: 320+ (recommended)
  • • Graduate GPA: 3.5+
  • • Research proposal (2-3 pages)
  • • Academic CV with publications
  • • 4 Recommendation letters
